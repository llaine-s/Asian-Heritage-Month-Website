Mani Jassal, born of Indian descent, represents both the exotic designs of India and the unique styles of North America. Her goal, expressed in the Mani Jassal brand, delivers bridal and evening wear that highlights the user's current identity and merges it with their traditional background. Her brand allows customers to express their strengths and beauties without the fear of casting aside their ethnicity and cultural identity. Her creative and minimalist designs abolishes borders between ethnic and casual clothing and establishes a style for people with Jassal's background to enjoy. Jassal has brought about the joy and comfort of many individuals, both old and young, providing them with unique, unorthodox attire that accentuates their character and nature.
