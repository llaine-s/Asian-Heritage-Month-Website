Mani Jassal, a successful fashion designer, is the owner of the Mani Jassal brand, a luxury evening wear and bridal brand that features authentic and elaborate-styled clothes inspired by South Asian attire. The Mani Jassal brand has released 10 collections with pieces featured in tv shows, music videos and red carpets on celebrities and influencers including Madison Beer, Bebe Rexha, Diipa Khosla, Kiran Rai and Hannah Simone.
